10/6/22

So far the Scrimba course on React is pretty good. It's a very neat setup where you learn alongside an instructor. The lessons are video based, but happens in an editor environment. At any time you can pause the video, edit the code yourself to play around with it, and Scrimba notes the point in the video for you where changes were made. When you resume the video the code picks up where the instructor left off, but saves your edits in a timeline. What incredible technology. I may have to play with more Scrimba courses in the future. However in trying to follow along in my local environment (VSCode rather than just coding in the Scrimba editor), some things are getting lost in translation. Components won't render, imports don't seem to be working for me. I'll have to ask about this during squads this week. In the meantime I'm grasping the syntax and how it all works just fine. But it would be nice to see the results working in real time for me just like it's working in the instruction vids. Head down, keep swinging.
